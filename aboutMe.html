More Info about me


My name is Mees Dekker, I am twenty-four years old, and I live in Heerhugowaard, The Netherlands.

I am a junior game developer, currently studying Game Development at HvA in Amsterdam.

I`m really interested in games and apps that are made with technologies such as VR and AR. Seeing my code work, or something as simple as fixing an error or bug is the most satisfying thing to me. And it gives me motivation to develop more!



I like working alone but I prefer to work in a team. I think that communication is very important, so I like to encourage my team, support and motivate them the best way I can.

I got into the gaming world when I was still young, playing games such as Lego Star Wars or Mario Kart with my friends. I started to fantasize about how the game would turn out if “this” or “that” happened. And now that I’m learning how to make my fantasies come true I get more and more excited!

Besides school I play football pretty competitively, read some manga, study Japanese or play games with my friends.
