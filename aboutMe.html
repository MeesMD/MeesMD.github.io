More Info about me


My name is Mees Dekker, I am twenty-five years old, living in Heerhugowaard, The Netherlands.

I am a junior game developer, who graduated in Game Development at both Media College and HvA in Amsterdam.

I`m really interested in games and apps that are made with Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality, where I have also made personal projects but also projects on a professional level with these technologies.



I like working alone but I prefer to work in a team. I think that communication is key, so I like to encourage my team, support and motivate them the best way I can.

My love for games started when I was still young, playing games such as Lego Star Wars or Mario Kart with my friends. I'll always remember how fun and exciting it was playing back then, and even to this day I still get these feelings. That's why I strive to try and give people the same feeling I get when playing games.

Beside this, in my free time, I play football pretty competitively, read some manga, play games with my friends or study Japanese. As it is a dream of mine to eventually live and work there.
