Elemental Dude

About the project:

This was a project for school where we had to create a game with a team op developers. For this project we not only had to develop a game but also make devlogs, trailers and in the end also publish the game on itch.io.

This game is a third person roguelike where you defeat monsters to obtain their elements and use them to fight other elemental enemies. At the end of each level you will meet the boss monster that has different attack patterns depending on what element the boss is. Defeat enemies, get stronger and use your elements to survive!

My Contribution:

  • I made the Audio Manager so you can easily add and play sounds.
  • I made the Weapons Manager to handle all the weapons in terms of shooting, switching weapons, bullet impacts etc...
  • I contributed to the level design and creation of the levels.
  • And I edited all the trailers and devlogs for the Youtube page.
