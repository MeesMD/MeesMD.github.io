AR Volvo

About the project:

This is a project that I made for the car company Volvo. This project was made in SparkAR so you can use as a filter for either Facebook or Instagram.

You can place the car on any horizontal surface by tapping on the screen. The car will appear through a series of animations starting from a sketch of the outline car to the final car itself. After that you can slide through bullet points which each show a different aspect of the car.

My Contribution:

  • I made it possible to place the car on a horizontal surface.
  • I made some animations and timed them all correctly.
  • I made the bullet point slider.
  • And I made all the functions that happen when you sellect a certain bullet point like: rotating the car, fade in the highlight lines, fade in the text etc.
